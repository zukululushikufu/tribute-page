Timeline of Hirohiko Araki's prominent work

  • 1980 - Araki's debut in the manga world with Poker Under Arms
  • 1986 - The first volume of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is published
  • 1989 - Stardust Crusaders arc begins
  • 1992 - Dio is defeated and Diamond is Unbreakable begins
  • 1995 - Giorno Giovanna has a dream and thus begins the Golden Wind arc
  • 2000 - Diavalo gets Gold Experienced and the Stone Ocean arc starts
  • 2004 - The JoJo universe is reset and Araki gets really into horse racing with the Steel Ball Run arc
  • 2011 - The infinite spin defeats the president of the United States and a paraplegic jockey walks once more
  • 2013 - JoJo's Bizarre Adventure receives an anime adaptation
